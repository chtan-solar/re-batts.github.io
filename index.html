🌍 The Problem

Global lithium resources are finite and expected to face shortages by 2050. At the same time, electronic waste is rapidly increasing worldwide.

Without sustainable recycling solutions, the transition to renewable energy will face critical bottlenecks.

💡 Our Solution

ReBatts delivers cutting-edge lithium-ion battery recycling using:

  • Hydrometallurgical processes (low energy, high efficiency)
  • Electrochemical recovery technologies

Our goal: 100% recovery of valuable materials with minimal environmental impact.

⚙️ Why Not Conventional Methods?

Traditional pyrometallurgy is:

  • Energy intensive
  • Expensive
  • Environmentally harmful

ReBatts provides a cleaner, more efficient alternative.

🚀 Technology Advantage

Our electrochemical approach leverages advanced membrane and ion-selective systems to:

  • Increase lithium recovery efficiency
  • Reduce energy consumption
  • Enable scalable industrial deployment
